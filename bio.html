BIO
Rey Melendez, is a Puerto Rican Artist born in 1987. The moment he graduated from Miami Jackson Sr. High Rey enlisted in the United States armed forces, specifically the United States Marines. During this eight year patriotic endeavor Rey was met with many hardships and life changing experiences that have helped mold his ideals. Mainly working with spray paint with a bright color pallet, he creates art that is meant to take your mind from those dark places, to a colorful world. This world of color is intended to be pleasing to the senses. These creations are an escape from the dark little places that reside in our minds.
